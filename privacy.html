Three Singsters Poster — Privacy Policy

Three Singsters Poster is a local desktop/studio tool used by the Three Singsters creators to upload short videos to their own TikTok, YouTube, Instagram, and Facebook accounts via official platform APIs.

What we store

  • OAuth tokens (access/refresh) stored only on the creator’s computer in a local configuration file.
  • Video and image files stored locally on the creator’s computer.

What we don’t do

  • We do not sell personal data.
  • We do not share tokens with third parties other than the official APIs of TikTok, Google/YouTube, and Meta when posting.
